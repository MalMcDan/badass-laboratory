Why this exists.

Everything here starts the same way: I run into a problem in my own life, and I build something to solve it.

Badass Laboratory is not about hustle culture, perfection, or becoming a productivity superhero.

It is about making ordinary life feel a little less overwhelming.

If something I make helps someone else feel more organized, more capable, or more at home in their own life, that is the best outcome I could ask for.

Current experiments.

These are active builds, not polished corporate products. They are tools being shaped by actual use, actual frustration, and the hope that practical software can still feel cozy, useful, and a little bit magical.

What “badass” means here.

Being a badass is not about climbing Mount Everest.

It is remembering to renew your insurance. Planning dinner after work. Getting back on the motorcycle. Caring for your family. Surviving difficult seasons and slowly building something better.

Those are the victories this workshop is built for.
