Why this exists

Everything here starts the same way.

I run into a problem in my own life.

I build something to solve it.

If it ends up helping someone else too, that's the best outcome I could ask for.

Badass Laboratory isn't about hustle culture or becoming a productivity superhero.

It's about making ordinary life feel a little less overwhelming.

What "Badass" means here

Being a badass isn't about climbing Mount Everest.

It's remembering to renew your insurance.

It's planning dinner after work.

It's getting back on the motorcycle.

It's caring for your family.

It's surviving difficult seasons and slowly building something better.

Those are the victories this workshop is built for.
