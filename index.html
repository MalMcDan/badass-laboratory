Why this exists.

Most software asks people to do more: more notifications, more dashboards, more tasks, more systems to maintain.

I am interested in something quieter.

I want software that helps people spend less energy keeping life together, so they have more room for the things that actually matter: riding, family, dinner, hobbies, rest, and being present in the life they already have.

Badass here does not mean climbing Mount Everest. It means handling ordinary Tuesdays a little more gracefully.

What’s on the workbench.

These are active experiments, not polished corporate products. Each one started as a real-life problem and is being shaped into something useful.

Can I build something for you?

Sometimes people see something I have built and realize they have a similar problem. If your life, work, family, or small business has a repetitive task that feels harder than it should, I can help think through it.
